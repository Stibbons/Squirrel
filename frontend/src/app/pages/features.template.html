Meet Squirrel

What is Squirrel?

Squirrel is your swissknife of your investment life. It is an online tool gathering everything you need to find, decide, follow your investment.

But there plenty of similar tool online?

Most of the online portfolio managers offer you access to an automated investment strategy, with no effort from your side. Enjoy the best algorithms that make wonders, in the finance, work for you. Squirrel does not make these promises. What it does promise is to give you the best tool to track you portfolio and find your own strategy.

What makes it different from other online portfolio managers?

Before everything else, Squirrel is a tool aimed at guiding you from day 1 when your start you investment journey, to build step by step your own portfolio. You have all the meaningful information in front of you, in your Squirrel Dashboard.

There is no mystic algorithm behind Squirrel, no Award winning Trading Software that will make you rich. Just well crafted tools, specially targeted to private individual.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions about Squirrel?

General

A quite ambitious project coming from a modest idea born early 2014.

Hi! My name is Gaetan Semet. I am Software engineer who started a very humble investor life and was very disappointed with existing tools to follow my different portfolio.

As humble as my investments was, I had to open several broker accounts to exploit the best of each of them. My first broker was quite good for buying stock from my home country (France), but was over expensive for stocks abroad. I then opened another account on a cheaper broker.

I also wanted to follow my Mutual Funds. I didn't understood why they would be treated differently than stocks.

I started tracking my different stock and mutual funds portfolio using a speadsheet software. But I felt very restricted when using such software, without beneficting from modern tool, dynamic and interactives charts such as what we can see on the web.

So I started using Google Spreadsheet to follow my investments, and I felt in love with its the 100% online nature. No matter which day of the week we are, if I am at home or on the go, I could see and edit my portfolio instantanously.

But when data starts to becomes quite heavy, with lot of custom import scripts, the Speadsheet started slowing down and become less confortable to work with.

I was also disappointed by the graphs themselves, they were not customable enough and not so good looking, compared to graph produced by JavaScript libraries such as HighCharts.

Squirrel started as a personal work but I wanted to do it the right way, with cutting edge web technologies. Just in case I could release it as a real service.
